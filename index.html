More about Edzeeta Learning!!!

I dedicated most of my undergraduate journey to learn the basics of research in the field of health and education which resulted in three publications. Edzeeta Learning, an edtech startup by alumni of NITK and MSRIT, was born out of an urge to have a productive lockdown period.
